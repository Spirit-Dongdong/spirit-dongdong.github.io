grep匹配tab键

grep在匹配的字段中还有tab键时,应该使用如下方式:

grep “xxx”$’\t’”yyy” filename

如以下的日志记录,想找出detailconversioned:true loginUid:0的记录,

cookied=05dvOVL/KSZYfF/3Em8SAg 4 1 79975758759941 sort:resumeclick^desc timediff:436183 clicked:true detailconversioned:false loginUid:0

cookied=05dvOVL4S14BIwhUYGiSAg 4 21 8010957718758 sort:resumeclick^desc timediff:263 clicked:true detailconversioned:true loginUid:0

cookied=05dvOVL4S14BIwhUYGiSAg 4 21 8010957718758 sort:resumeclick^desc timediff:263 clicked:true detailconversioned:true loginUid:136784352

则用

grep ‘detailconversioned:true’$’\t’’loginUid:0’ logfile
